Hi, I'm [Your Name] — I take pictures on my phone.

Not because it's trendy, but because it's the camera I always have. Everything on this site was shot, and mostly edited, on a phone screen.

Philosophy

I started paying closer attention to light and framing long before I owned anything you'd call "real" camera equipment. The phone in my pocket turned out to be enough — it's always there, it's quick, and it disappears into the moment instead of getting in the way of it.

What I care about is composition: where the eye lands first, what's left out of frame, how a shadow or a reflection changes a plain scene into something worth a second look. Equipment can help, but it was never the thing holding the pictures back.

This site is a growing collection of those moments, loosely grouped so you can browse by mood rather than by date.
